Here is information on my research of Webhost sites.

My first webhost is GoDaddy

Go Daddy is user-friendly, cheap to use and has a variety of different templates.

It starts out at as little as $3.66/month with the ability to easily upgrade if you want.

Go Daddy's speed is fast and their customer service is available 24/7.

My second webhost is Fatcow

Fatcow's website design is nice for first time users because they offer a free domain name, unlimited space, bandwidth, emails, and databases.

Fatcow offers a unique feature that could be something users choose, is that it is powered 100% by wind energy.

My third webhost is Wix

Wix would be a preferable webhost to use because of its' user-friendliness. Wix utilizes a simple drag and drop method which makes it ideal for all users.

Wix starts out at $4.08/month. The option to upgrade is available on one of their premium plans which lets you have your own domain name, remove ads, and offers a wider variety of templates.

Go Daddy

I chose Go Daddy because it is a well-known site with good quality customer service. I also think it will be the most user-friendly for what I am trying to do.

I think if I were designing a more intricate website I might choose someting with better features but for the fact I am a beginner and just starting up, Go Daddy will be the webhost provider for me.
